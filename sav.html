Savaya

Strategy Designer & Sneakerhead

About

Savaya Shinkaruk always knew she was meant to apply innovation to the qualities and definitions of traditional design. With a well-rounded portfolio in lifestyle and fashion work, she is always ready for a challenge to work with any company or brand. She earned her BA in Communications with a Major in Journalism, and is now attending OCAD University to attain her Masters Degree in Design. Her experience is in graphic design, UX/UI design, and print design. Her passion is in all things sneakers, but mainly to redefine, approach, and bridge innovation, research, management, and design.
